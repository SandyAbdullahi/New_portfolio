How I turn ideas into shipped product

A four-step, heuristic-driven workflow that balances speed, clarity, and craft.

Step 1 of 4 End

Discover

Align on goals, audiences, and constraints. Speak the user's language and surface the real jobs-to-be-done.

  • 🧭 Match real world: task language, mental models
  • 🪟 Visibility: clear next steps & expectations
  • 🧩 Consistency: align terms across touchpoints
  • 🧠 Recognition over recall: visible choices
Methods
  • Stakeholder interviews & competitive scans
  • Outcome mapping & success metrics
  • IA sketching, flows, and content inventory

Design

Type-led systems, grid discipline, and components that scale. Minimalist, but expressive where it matters.

  • ✂️ Minimalist: remove noise, keep signal
  • 🚪 User control: undo, cancel, safe exits
  • 🛡️ Error prevention: defaults, constraints
  • 🧩 Consistency: tokens & patterns
Artifacts
  • Responsive type scale & spacing
  • Accessible color system (AA+)
  • Component spec with variants & states

Build

Robust front-ends, typed APIs, and smooth integrations. Performance first, then polish.

  • ⚡ Efficiency: shortcuts & power-user paths
  • ♻️ Consistency/standards: semantics & ARIA
  • 🧯 Error prevention: form guards & safe ops
  • 📣 Status: loading, success, and failure states
Tech
  • Three.js + GSAP micro-interactions
  • CI/CD, analytics, and perf budgets
  • Accessibility checks baked into pipeline

Validate & Ship

Test with users, instrument the experience, and ship safely with rollbacks and alerts.

  • 🧪 Help & documentation: inline hints
  • ↩️ User freedom: easy exits & redo
  • 📈 Status: progress, errors, recovery
  • 🔁 Iteration: telemetry-driven improvements
Validation
  • Usability tests & success task rates
  • Real-world performance (Core Web Vitals)
  • Staged rollouts & monitoring
