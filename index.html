Process

A brutalist lens through NN/g’s ten heuristics.

  1. 01

    System Status, Always

    Explicit loading, progress, and success/failure states. Nothing hidden, nothing implied.

  2. 02

    Speak Human, Not Jargon

    Words that read like you talk. Labels in plain English beat clever tooltips.

  3. 03

    User Control > Wizardry

    Undo, redo, and safe exits. Modals and destructive actions follow strict patterns.

  4. 04

    Consistency is a Feature

    Grids, rhythm, and tokenized decisions. Components don’t freelance.

  5. 05

    Error Prevention

    Constrain input, pre-validate, confirm dangerous paths. UIs that make mistakes hard.
